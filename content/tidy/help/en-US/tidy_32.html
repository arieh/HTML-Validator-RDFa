"<" + "/" + letter not allowed here

Cause:

The 2 characters "</" have been detected in a wrong place.

Example:

In most cases, this is due to wrong javascript:

document.write("</h1>");
document.write("<\/h1>");

Solution:

Add a "\" in the javascript code.
